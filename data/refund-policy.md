# BajiMart Return and Refund Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-09

This policy is adapted from the public return-policy patterns used by large U.S. retailers, especially Target's public stance on denying returns for suspected fraud or abuse and on excluding certain non-returnable items. BajiMart applies this policy strictly for automated decisions.

## 1. Identity and Order Ownership

1. The customer must provide either an email address or a valid order ID.
2. The order must exist in the CRM.
3. If both an email address and order ID are provided, the order must belong to that customer.
4. Guest orders are not eligible for automated approval unless the CRM can match the order and customer identity.

## 2. General Return Window

1. Most physical items are eligible for refund only within 90 calendar days of delivery.
2. Damaged, defective, missing, or wrong-item claims may receive tighter review and must still be within the allowed return window.
3. Orders still in transit are not eligible for automated refund unless they are merchant-cancelled or otherwise clearly refundable.
4. BajiMart may deny returns, refunds, or exchanges when fraud, suspected fraud, or abuse is detected.

## 3. Payment and Fraud Rules

1. The order payment must be captured or settled.
2. Open chargebacks block automated refunds.
3. Refund amount must not exceed the paid order total minus prior refunds.
4. Rapid refund activity, high fraud scores, or other risk signals may trigger manual review.

## 4. Non-Returnable Categories

1. Final-sale items are never eligible for automated refunds.
2. Gift cards and similar stored-value products are never eligible for cash refunds.
3. Digital downloads are not returnable once delivered or used, unless a policy exception clearly applies.
4. Opened hygiene items, consumables, and perishables are not refundable unless the claim is verified damaged on arrival.
5. Specialty or partner-only marketplace items may be subject to additional restrictions.

## 5. Special Case Handling

1. Subscriptions and recurring billing issues may be approved only when the charge is a duplicate, billing error, or otherwise clearly refundable.
2. Bundles may be refunded only as a full bundle unless item-level prices exist and the policy allows a partial return.
3. Products returned outside the policy window or without the required CRM records are denied.

## 6. Risk Review

1. More than two refunds in the last 180 days requires manual review.
2. Fraud score of 70 or higher requires manual review.
3. VIP status does not override policy.
4. Missing required CRM data is treated as a denial for automated processing.

## 7. Required Outcome

The agent must return one of three outcomes:

1. APPROVED: all required checks pass and the refund is recorded.
2. DENIED: at least one strict policy rule fails.
3. MANUAL_REVIEW: identity and payment rules pass, but risk controls require a human decision.

The agent must tell the customer the decision, the primary policy reason, and the next action. Administrators may see an auditable summary of tool calls and policy checks, but the customer-facing response should stay concise and clear.
